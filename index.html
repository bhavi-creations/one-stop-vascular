Meet Dr. P C Gupta: Clinical Director and Head of Department for Vascular & Endovascular Surgery at CARE Hospitals

Dr. P C Gupta serves as the Clinical Director and Head of the Department for Vascular and Endovascular Surgery & Vascular IR at CARE Hospitals in Banjara Hills, India. Boasting three decades of experience in the medical field, specializing in vascular and endovascular surgery, he is widely recognized as a premier vascular specialist in Hyderabad. Dr. P C Gupta has successfully conducted numerous surgeries on a global scale, demonstrating unwavering dedication, passion, and a commitment to patient comfort. His approach has consistently yielded high success rates, earning admiration from all those he has treated.

A distinguished academic background compliments Dr. P C Gupta’s practical expertise. He earned his MBBS from the esteemed Armed Forces Medical College, Pune, and later completed his MS Surgery from PGIMER, Chandigarh. Further enhancing his skills, he pursued a fellowship in Vascular Surgery at Nagoya University School of Medicine, Japan. Driven by a quest for knowledge, he underwent specialized training in carotid surgery at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, USA, and complex aortic surgery at Methodist Hospital, Houston, USA.

Dr. P C Gupta

Clinical Director and HOD, Vascular and Endovascular Surgery & Vascular IR

Dr. P C Gupta, Clinical Director and HOD specializing in Vascular & Endovascular Surgery, brings 30 years of experience and is based at CARE Hospitals, Banjara Hills, Hyderabad, including the Outpatient Centre.

Dr. P C Gupta has made significant contributions to the scientific community, with over 25 publications in National and International Journals. Notable works include the documentation of the world’s first case of carotid aneurysms operated on during pregnancy, contributions to textbooks on various topics such as ischemia-reperfusion injury, varicose veins, neointimal hyperplasia, and stem cell therapy. He co-authored the Global Vascular Guidelines for the management of CLTI and serves on the Editorial Board of the Indian Journal of Vascular & Endovascular Surgery and the European Journal of Vascular & Endovascular Surgery. Additionally, he plays a crucial role as a teacher and examiner for DrNB in Vascular Surgery, with a particular focus on his expertise in Aortic and carotid surgery.

Currently, Dr. P C Gupta holds the position of President-Elect of the Vascular Society of India and serves as a council member of the World Federation of Vascular Societies, further solidifying his standing as a respected figure in the global vascular community.
